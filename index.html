Oncology in general and why is the AI included

Oncology, also called Carcinology, is a faculty in medicine. Mainly the goal is to research, investigatie and treat the cancer disease. They try to understand the mechanism of formation of the tumors, carzinomas, sarcomas etc. and seek the best means to fight it. Mostly it is done in the following four methods: chemotherapy, hormonetherapy, immunotherapy and specific cancer therapy. Why does the AI get included in oncology, and medicine in general? The AI has a bright future in the medical field. It has the capability to fundamentally transform medicine. These AI's can provide great support, especially in the oncology. AI have a quick understanding how cancer cells become resisstant to their drug, which can help improve the development and adjust the usage of the drug.
