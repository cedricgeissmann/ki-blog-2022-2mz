Medicine and oncology and why is the AI included

Oncology, also called Carcinology, is a faculty in medicine. Mainly the goal is to research, investigatie and treat the cancer disease. They try to understand the mechanism of formation of the tumors, carzinomas, sarcomas etc. and seek the best means to fight it. Mostly it is done in the following four methods: chemotherapy, hormonetherapy, immunotherapy and specific cancer therapy. The most common, that we are going to take a look at afterwards is chemotherapy. Why does the AI get included in oncology, and medicine in general? The AI has a bright future in the medical field. It has the capability to fundamentally transform medicine. These AI's can provide great support, especially in the oncology. AI have a quick understanding how cancer cells become resisstant to their drug, which can help improve the development and adjust the usage of the drug. So in this blog we are taking a look at the AI in chemotherapy, radiotherapy and the AI in supervising patients.

AI in the usage of chemotherapy

chemotherapy is the most known and most used cancer treatment. They include the usage of cytotoxic drugs. These drugs involve cell division that leads to the death of the cancer cells. As good as this sounds it has also a negative effect. They also attack the healthy body cells leading to side effects. The AI gets included in the chemotherapy because it adapts the treatment to the individual patient better. So this can not only reduce the side effect but it also can increase the effectiveness of the treatment. With that usage of AI the waste of medicine will drasticly decrease.
